About Us

Ours is a story of how much pride we have in raising generations that are climate conscience even as they eat.

Climgric grew from the need to promote health, agriculture and positive climate change within the East African region. This dream has been and is still being realized through our beautiful Orchids owned by small –holder farmers.

Who We Are

We are a chain of modern social farms that use hydroponics technology to grow berries in bulk. We work with young small holder farmers in rural areas to grow berries with love for you.

We are located a few kilometers outside the beautiful City of Nairobi. Our mission is to provide you, our client, with the best quality fruits while using the most environmentally friendly agricultural growing practices

The Problem

The unpredicted climate conditions and the high cost of farm inputs have left East African small holder farmers experiencing losses. Dispersion of the small holder farmers increases their production costs and reduces their competitiveness in the market.

70% of all farmers in sub-Saharan Africa are small holder farmers yet Africa’s full agricultural potential remains untapped.

Our Solution

We provide fresh berry fruits year long in bulk to the African Market.

  • 2 week boot camp on berry farming using hydroponics to small holder farmers.
  • Link to finances by setting up hydroponics and providing seedlings needed.
  • Increase the market power for small holder farmers by linking them directly to berry market.

Our Impact

Growing using hydroponics increases productivity and maximizes on land use.

We are connecting local small holder farmers to direct market and boosting local markets.

We are using technology to design a sustainable way to grow fruits while preserving the environment and natural resources.
