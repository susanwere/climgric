About Us

Ours is a story of how much pride we have in raising generations that are climate conscience even as they eat.

ClimGric grew from the need to promote health ,agriculture and positive climate change within the East African region. This dream has been and is still being realized through our beautiful Orchad.

Who We Are

We are a modern fruit orchard located a few kilometers outside the beautiful City of Nairobi. Our mission is to provide you, our client, with the best quality fruits while using the most environmentally friendly agricultural growing practices
