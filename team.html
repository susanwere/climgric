Why choose us?

ClimGric grew from the need to promote health ,agriculture and positive climate change within the East African region.

Ours is a story of how much pride we have in raising generations that are climate conscience even as they eat. It is a tale of freshness, health and beauty all blended together to form a mocktail of goodness ”Shilayi”.

Customers

Our Berries are currently being enjoyed widely by our esteemed customers who are mainly in the manufacturing and hospitality sectors.

Location

ClimGric Farms are beautifully spread in Konza, Kisumu,Busia and Nyahururu regions in Kenya.We get to enjoy different climates for this regions that plant different fruits.

Environment Conservation

Our mission is to provide you, our client, with the best quality fruits while using the most environmentally friendly agricultural growing practices. We operate a modern hydrponics farm that ensures all our clients have fruits every season of the year.

Team

We are a family of farmers, each with unique personalities and talents. Our people are our most important resource. Our farm’s success is due to our passionate, driven people who work hard for a common goal to produce some of the best quality fruits in East Africa. Living by the motto “Shilayi-meaning goodness” drives us to continuously improve in our practices and to be more innovative.
